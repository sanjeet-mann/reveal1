Challenges

  • Share findings with campus and colleagues
  • Purchase missing titles
  • Adjust demand driven acquisition (DDA) parameters
  • Set goals for collecting titles by authors of color

“Efforts to gain a social justice mindset and to understand issues of Diversity, Equity and Inclusion (DEI) take time, effort and resources. They must be nurtured and given attention.”

Monroe-Gulick and Morris 2023, p. 18

Outcomes

  • Share findings with campus and colleagues - PARTIALLY DONE
  • Purchase missing titles - MOSTLY DONE
  • Adjust demand driven acquisition (DDA) parameters - DONE
  • Set goals for collecting titles by authors of color - NOT DONE

“Cost saving strategies … have largely favored vendor relationships over investing in collective effort among libraries and are part and parcel of a larger dynamic of business logic within higher education that privileges demand and immediate need over long-term support for scholarship and equity.”

Jahnke, Tanaka and Palazzolo 2022, p. 179
