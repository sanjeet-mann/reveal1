What is Office 365?

Software

Files

People

Office 365 includes cloud versions of Microsoft software, as well as Sharepoint, which is made up of buckets of data files plus a directory of people, who are sorted into groups and given access permissions to see and do things.

Why this topic?

I chose this topic because I was curious about what is in Office 365. My goal is to inform and get us thinking about possible uses for the software, but not "sell" it as a solution.

The choice of whether or not to use technology should follow from your needs and the capabilities of the technology. For example, I use paper planner and notebooks because I found I remember things better when I physically write them down.

The best way to learn technology is to try it for something that you're already interested in, something low-stakes that you already need to do.

Project management

Project management is structuring and sequencing work and coordinating people in order to accomplish something exceptional within constraints of limited time, money and scope.

Project management involves a mindset of being strategic to accomplish a complex goal - something "bigger" than everyday work that requires teamwork.
